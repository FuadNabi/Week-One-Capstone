Conference
"The Importance of
a Healthy Lifestyle"(TIHL)

At this conference, we will discuss the importance of a healthy lifestyle and how it will change your life.

2023.01.01(Sun) ~ 02(Mon)

@ Heydar Aliyev Palace, Baku, Azerbaijan

Program Topics

Healthy Life

What are a healthy lifestyle and its importance of it. What will change in our life after this

Family and Health.

We will talk about, how a healthy lifestyle will affect your family.

Bad Habits

We will touch on the topic of bad habits and how to deal with them, how your life will change after that

Our Children

The role of the volunteer movement in the formation of a healthy lifestyle among young people.

Traditional Medicine

The importance of traditional medicine and its role in shaping a healthy lifestyle.

Famous Speakers

Keith Ablow

Clinical professor at Tufts University School of Medicine,

Doctor of Medicine degree from Johns Hopkins Medical School and England Medical Center.

Daniel Amen

American celebrity doctor who practices as a psychiatrist specialist as director of the Amen Clinics

Amen is board certified by the American Board of Psychiatry with a subspecialty in Child and Adolescent Psychiatry.

Nancy Coover Andreasen

Director of Andrew H. Woods Chair of Psychiatry at the Roy J. and Lucille A. Carver College

She received her undergraduate degree from the University of Nebraska with majors in English, Psychology, and Philosophy

David Ames

Emeritus Professor in the Department of Psychiatry at the University of Melbourne

Ames graduated with honours in Medicine and Obstetrics & Gynaecology (MBBS) in 1978 from the University of Melbourne.

Susan Bailey

Professor of Child Mental Health at the University of Central Lancashire

She studied medicine at the University of Manchester and graduated with Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MB ChB) degrees in 1973.

Abuzar Komurchu

Professor and Chairman of the Department of Psychiatry at the Weill Cornell Medical College of Cornell University

He was formerly the Nancy Friend Pritzker professor in psychiatry at Stanford University and dean of research and neuroscience at the University of California, Los Angeles School of Medicine.
